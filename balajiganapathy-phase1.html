My Journey

I'm a transformational C-level executive who harnesses the convergence of purpose, people, and technology to advance equity, access, and sustainability at scale. With 25+ years of progressive leadership in corporate responsibility, philanthropy, and social innovation, I mobilize cross-sector ecosystems to solve complex societal challenges while driving sustainable business growth.

My journey began with a simple dream during my engineering days—a hydrogen fuel cell-powered vehicle that would emit clean drinking water instead of carbon. That vision crystallized my lifelong commitment to bridging technology and social good, evolving from entrepreneurship to organizational leadership and ultimately to architecting transformational impact at scale.
